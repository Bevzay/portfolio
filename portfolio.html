MQ Tooling Ltd.

MQ Tooling Ltd. is a small business in New Zealand that supplies engineering tools to businesses or individuals who have an interest in such tools. The founder approached Manaaki for help with the company's website — revamping the UX/UI design of it — and I accepted to lend a hand.

This project is simple, yet I am not experienced in Shopify web development. To help relieve my lack of experience, I used one of Shopify's free themes and altered it with their drag-and-drop feature on the homepage. With the other pages, however, I've used my frontend development skills to modify the texts into the table/boxes properly and fixed some links to either open to a new tab or to the current tab.
